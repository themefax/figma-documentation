General Infromation

Maxdino - is IT service Figma template can be customized easily to suit your wishes. Just change your text, image, colors and get your website. All images are used for demo purposes only, they are not included into the download package.

What you can do with Yoda?

This is a beautiful Admin template UI Kit based on Ant Design. Comes with 30+ special card & chart designs, functional components, lots of reusable and customizable UI Style. Every component is crafted with love to speed up your workflow.

How figma files structured?

It’s has a simple and fully scalable file structure organization based on Ant Design System. Components are grouped by their function instead of by their complexity.

Discover Design Styles

In the far right panel on Figma you can find all the styles available in the project. You can access text, color, and shadow styles & all the layouts you need from there. Font styles are created according to Ant-Design standards.
