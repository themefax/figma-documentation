General Infromation

Maxdino - is Creative Agency Figma template can be customized easily to suit your wishes. Just change your text, image, colors and get your website. All images are used for demo purposes only, they are not included into the download package.

What you can do with Maxdino?

This is a beautiful multiporpose figma template for creative agency. There are 28 inner pages including three homepages.Also there are all pages related to selling digital services with pricing plans.Moreover, beautiful user dashboard design, customizable components that you can change as you wish.

How figma files structured?

It’s has a simple and customizable Creative agency template. You can use it for selling your digital service and startup. Components are grouped by their function instead of by their complexity.

Discover Design Styles

In the far right panel on Figma you can find all the styles available in the project. You can access text and color, & all the layouts you need from there. There is a grid system for the convenience of developers and to ensure that the design looks right on different screens.

Edit Styles
